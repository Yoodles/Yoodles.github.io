How To Play

Change the word one move at a time to get from the top to the bottom.

Moves

  • Each move must be a valid word.
  • Change a letter:
    W O R D → W A R D
  • Add/remove a letter:
    W O R D → W O R L D
    W A R D → W A R
  • Jumble the letters:
    W O R D S → S W O R D
    W A R D → D R A W
